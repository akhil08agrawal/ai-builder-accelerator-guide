# Soma Capital Fellowship — Application Questions

**Program:** $100K uncapped SAFE, 0% equity
**Duration:** Ongoing support
**Apply at:** https://somafellows.com or email fellows@somacap.com

## Questions / Sections

1. **About Us: Who We Are** — Personal narrative for each founder:
   - Written as a story, not a resume
   - Specific moments that reveal character (failures, pivots, hustle moments)
   - How you found each other and how you work together
   - What each person does day-to-day
   - Development tools and workflow

2. **What We're Building** — Product description:
   - The problem in concrete terms
   - How the product works
   - Key product metaphors or analogies
   - Show you use your own product daily

3. **Why We Can Build This** — Team-problem fit:
   - Why your specific backgrounds make you uniquely qualified
   - Technical depth (not just "we're smart" — show specific systems you've built)
   - User research depth

4. **Current Stage & Traction** — Metrics:
   - Users, waitlist, grants, downloads
   - Interviews and community validation
   - Revenue status
   - Time spent building

5. **Our Vision** — Long-term view:
   - Where knowledge work is headed
   - Your near-term ICP and why you start there
   - Expansion path to other verticals

6. **Why Now** — Timing argument:
   - What converging trends make this the right moment
   - Why this wasn't possible 2 years ago
   - Why waiting would be too late

7. **Team** — Structured info:
   - Roles, education, full-time dates
   - Equity split
   - Three people you'd recruit regardless of what you build (with personal stories for each)

8. **Why Soma** — Specific alignment:
   - Reference their thesis ("software automating the world")
   - Reference specific portfolio companies they've backed
   - What you'd specifically use Soma for (not generic "mentorship")
   - Specific Day 1 asks ready
   - Reference their YC prep support (130+ YC companies backed)

9. **How We Heard About Soma** — Personal discovery story (must be genuine, not AI-generated)

10. **Links** — Website, demo video, founder video, location

## Notes

- The tone of this application is **story-driven**. Write it like you're telling a friend about your startup over coffee.
- Soma's terms are the most founder-friendly: $100K, uncapped SAFE, 0% equity.
- They commit and wire same day. Speed is part of their culture — match it.
- Soma actively helps fellows prepare for YC (130+ YC companies in portfolio). Mention YC plans if applicable.
- Have your "three people you'd recruit" section ready with genuine personal stories, not just impressive credentials.
- The "How We Heard About Soma" section must be a personal, authentic story.
